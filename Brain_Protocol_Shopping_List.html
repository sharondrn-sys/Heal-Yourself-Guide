Phase 02 Weeks 5–16 Aluminium Removal & Dental Detox

Aluminium accumulates in brain tissue over decades — from cookware, deodorants, vaccines adjuvants, and food additives. Professor Christopher Exley's work at Keele University documents its direct neurotoxicity. Silica is the primary documented removal agent — it binds aluminium ions and facilitates renal excretion. ACV mobilises; silica binds and removes. Both are required together.

Silica Sources — Use One Primary + One Budget Backup
BioSil — Ch-OSA Orthosilicic Acidprimary choice
6–10 drops or 2 capsules daily · In water · Away from meals
Ch-OSA (choline-stabilised orthosilicic acid) is the only form shown in published trials to cross the blood-brain barrier and bind aluminium ions directly. This is the form used in all of Christopher Exley's research. BioSil is the original patented source. More expensive than alternatives but clinically validated.
⚠️ Ensure 2+ litres of water daily — silica needs water to flush aluminium through the kidneys. Without adequate hydration, mobilisation without elimination occurs.

500mg · 1–2× daily · Standardised to 7% silica
Highest plant source of bioavailable silica. A portion converts to orthosilicic acid in the gut. Not as potent as Ch-OSA but significantly cheaper and still effective for long-term maintenance. Use as a budget alternative or alongside Ch-OSA for higher total silica load.
⚠️ Avoid in kidney disease or kidney stones. Use supplement form only — the unprocessed plant contains thiaminase which destroys vitamin B1.

Food-Grade Diatomaceous Earthcheapest option
½–1 tsp in water · Morning · Empty stomach · Start with ¼ tsp and build over 2 weeks
~85% amorphous silica. Cheapest silica source available. Also provides documented gut integrity benefits — the silica matrix physically cleans intestinal walls. Additional documented heavy metal chelation. Food-grade only — industrial or pool-grade diatomaceous earth is a respiratory hazard.
⚠️ Food-grade ONLY — never pool, filter, or industrial grade. Do not inhale the powder at any time — crystalline silica is a lung hazard even in food-grade form if inhaled.

1–2 tbsp in large glass of water · Daily · With or before silica supplement
ACV = Apple Cider Vinegar — raw, unfiltered, with the "mother." Malic acid content mobilises aluminium from soft tissue and bone. Critical rule: never use ACV alone — always pair with silica to bind what is mobilised. Mobilising without binding causes redistribution to other tissues, not elimination.
⚠️ Always dilute in at least 250ml water — undiluted ACV damages tooth enamel and the oesophagus. Drink through a straw if possible. Do not use with existing low potassium or kidney issues without checking with a practitioner.
