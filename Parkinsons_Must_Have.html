If budget is tight — these are the items you cannot skip. Everything else helps. These are the foundation.

How to use this list

This is not the full protocol — it is the minimum viable protocol. Every item here was chosen because removing it would either break the phase mechanism entirely, or directly harm the healing process. These are non-negotiable.

Items marked budget-friendly cost very little — they should never be cut regardless of budget. Items marked core are the primary mechanism of that phase. Items marked Parkinson's are specific to dopamine and neurological function — cutting them defeats the purpose of the protocol entirely.

Items in the "can wait" section are genuinely valuable — add them as soon as budget allows. They are not on this list because they are unimportant. They are off this list because the must-haves cannot work without them.

Phase 1 Weeks 1–2 Open the Drainage Doors

The colon must move daily. The liver must be protected. The lymph must be activated. Without these, everything added in Phase 2 either recirculates as toxin or fails to absorb. This phase is the most affordable of all — most items cost almost nothing.

300–400mg every night before bed
The colon must move every day. One blocked day during detox means toxins recirculate directly into the bloodstream. Magnesium citrate is the safest, gentlest daily colon support available and costs almost nothing.
