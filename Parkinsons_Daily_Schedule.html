Phase 1 Weeks 1–2 · Days 1–14 Open the Drainage Doors

Prepare the Body. No Supplements Yet.

The body's five exit routes — colon, liver, kidneys, lymph, skin — must be open before anything else is added. Putting supplements into a congested body is like pouring clean water into a blocked drain. These two weeks are the foundation everything else is built on.

What You May Feel This Week
Days 1–3: Possibly nothing, or mild fatigue as the body shifts gears.
Days 4–7: Possible headaches, skin breakouts, or stronger-smelling urine — this is the kidneys and skin waking up. It is a good sign.
Days 8–14: Better bowel movements, clearer head, improved energy in the morning. Sleep may already improve from the magnesium alone.
If feeling very unwell: Slow down. Drink more water. Add activated charcoal (500mg, away from everything). The body is clearing. Support it, don't push it.

Phase 2 Weeks 3–4 · Days 15–28 Neurological Stack Begins + Biofilm Disruption

The Brain Starts Getting Support.

Drainage is open. Now the core Parkinson's supplements begin. Simultaneously, biofilm disruption starts — breaking the protective shell around pathogens so the immune system can find them. Everything from Phase 1 continues. This is where the real work begins.

What You May Feel This Week
Days 15–18: Some people feel a mild energy lift within days of starting Mucuna — others take 1–2 weeks. Be patient. Start at 250mg if 500mg feels too strong.
Days 19–24: Possible mild nausea from Mucuna — always take 30 min before food, never on a full stomach. If nausea persists, reduce dose.
Biofilm die-off (Days 15+): Serrapeptase and Nattokinase may cause brief flu-like symptoms, bloating, or fatigue as biofilm breaks down. This is normal. Drink extra water. Add activated charcoal if needed.
Sleep: Magnesium is often felt within the first week — deeper sleep, less night waking, reduced tremor intensity.

Phase 3 Weeks 5–8 · Days 29–56 Antiparasitic Rotation

Clear What Has Been Hidden.

Everything from Phases 1 and 2 continues. Now add the antiparasitic rotation. Rotate herbs every 7–10 days — parasites adapt to a single herb but cannot adapt to a rotation. This phase is the most intensive. Expect die-off. Support the drainage system heavily.

What You May Feel This Week
Die-off (Herxheimer) reaction is normal and expected: Flu-like symptoms, fatigue, headaches, skin rashes, strong body odour, unusual bowel movements, temporary worsening of symptoms. This is not the herbs harming you — it is the pathogens dying and releasing toxins as they die.

Managing die-off: Drink 3+ litres of water. Take activated charcoal 500mg (away from supplements). Add an extra detox bath. Rest. Reduce herb dose if overwhelming — never stop entirely.

What should improve: Energy, clarity, reduced bloating, better bowel regularity, improved sleep. Many people notice the Mucuna working better as gut health improves — because gut permeability was blocking absorption.

Phase 4 Weeks 9–12 · Days 57–84 Heavy Metal Clearing — Aluminium Out

Pull the Metals From Deep Tissue.

Aluminium accumulation in the substantia nigra — the brain region that fails in Parkinson's — is documented in post-mortem studies. This phase pulls it out. All previous phases continue. Binders are added and must be taken at least 2 hours away from food and supplements. Silica water intensifies. Never mobilise metals without binders present.

What You May Feel This Week
Weeks 9–10: Chlorella may cause brief nausea at first — build up slowly from 500mg. Start small, increase over 2 weeks.
Brain fog or fatigue bursts: As metals mobilise from tissue, they temporarily increase in the bloodstream. This is why binders are essential — they capture what is mobilised before it recirculates.
What should be improving by now: Tremor frequency or intensity should show measurable change. Sleep quality should be significantly better. Bowel function should be regular and easy. Energy more stable through the day.
If not yet seeing change: Check Mucuna timing — is it being taken far enough from protein? Check bowel frequency — is the colon clearing daily? These are the two most common blockers.

Phase 6 Week 17 & Beyond · Forever Maintenance — This Is Now Your Life

This Is Not a Protocol Anymore. This Is How You Live.

The world has not changed. Toxic exposure continues. Dopamine neurons need daily support. The gut microbiome needs daily feeding. These are not things you do for 16 weeks — they are the conditions under which a body can maintain and regenerate. This page is your permanent daily rhythm.

What You Should Be Experiencing by Now
Motor symptoms: Measurable reduction in tremor frequency or intensity. Rigidity less severe in the mornings. Better gait stability. Fewer freezing episodes or shorter duration.

Digestion: Daily bowel movements. Reduced bloating. Better food tolerance.
Mood and energy: Noticeably more stable. Depression and anxiety reduced. More resilience.
If not seeing these changes: Do not give up. Return to Phase 1 and audit the foundations — is the colon moving daily? Is Mucuna taken correctly away from protein? Is silica water being taken? Is exercise happening every day? The foundations create the results.
