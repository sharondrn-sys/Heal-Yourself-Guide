⚠️ Do the Full Body Detox First

The Brain Protocol works significantly better after a Full Body Detox. When the gut is burdened with parasites, biofilm, and heavy metals, even the best brain supplements struggle to reach their target. The detox opens drainage pathways, clears the gut-brain axis, and removes the heavy metal load that drives neuroinflammation. If you have not done the Full Body Detox Protocol, start there. Return here after Phase 2 minimum — ideally after the full 90 days.

Minimum if budget is tight Lion's Mane · Omega-3 (triglyceride form) · Magnesium L-Threonate before bed · Morning sunlight · Daily aerobic exercise. These five alone will produce measurable change within 60–90 days. Add the rest as budget allows.
