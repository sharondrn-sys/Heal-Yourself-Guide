⚠️ Do the Full Body Detox First

The Brain Protocol works significantly better after a Full Body Detox. When the gut is burdened with parasites, biofilm, and heavy metals, even the best brain supplements struggle to reach their target. The detox opens drainage pathways, clears the gut-brain axis, and removes the heavy metal load that drives neuroinflammation. If you have not done the Full Body Detox Protocol, start there. Return here after Phase 2 minimum — ideally after the full 90 days.

Who This Is For
This protocol is for anyone experiencing cognitive or neurological symptoms
Symptoms this protocol addresses
  • Brain fog — difficulty thinking clearly or finding words
  • Memory decline — short-term and long-term
  • Poor concentration and mental fatigue
  • Anxiety, low mood, emotional dysregulation
  • Sleep disturbances and poor recovery
  • Early cognitive decline or neurological deterioration
  • Post-viral cognitive symptoms (long COVID brain fog)
  • Movement disorders — tremor, rigidity, balance issues
  • Autism spectrum support (gut-brain connection)
  • ADD/ADHD — attention and executive function
Who benefits most
  • Anyone over 40 — neurodegeneration begins silently decades before symptoms
  • People with a family history of neurological conditions
  • Anyone who has had prolonged stress, trauma, or infection
  • People with amalgam fillings or known heavy metal exposure
  • Anyone who has been on long-term medication
  • Caregivers managing a neurological condition in a loved one
  • People who feel "not as sharp" as they used to be
  • Those recovering from burnout or adrenal fatigue
Minimum if budget is tight Lion's Mane · Omega-3 (triglyceride form) · Magnesium L-Threonate before bed · Morning sunlight · Daily aerobic exercise. These five alone will produce measurable change within 60–90 days. Add the rest as budget allows.
