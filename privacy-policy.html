Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Heal Yourself Guide ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you visit this website at healyourselfguide.github.io/Heal-Yourself-Guide/.

This guide is an educational health resource run by an individual. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we handle data.

1. Information We Collect

Email address. If you choose to join our community newsletter, we collect your email address. This is the only personal information we actively collect. Email collection is handled through Mailchimp, a third-party email marketing service.

Usage data. Like most websites, basic usage information may be collected automatically — such as your browser type, the pages you visit, and the time you spend on the site. This is collected through standard web hosting and analytics tools and is not linked to your personal identity.

No cookies for tracking. We do not use cookies to track you across the internet or build advertising profiles. Any cookies present are functional (for example, session cookies used by third-party services).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Email address: To send you updates about new protocols, new research, and new content added to this guide. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.
  • Usage data: To understand how visitors use the site and improve its content and structure. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

3. Third-Party Services

Mailchimp. Our email newsletter is managed by Mailchimp (The Rocket Science Group LLC). When you subscribe, your email address is stored on Mailchimp's servers. Mailchimp's privacy policy is available at mailchimp.com/legal/privacy.

iHerb. This guide contains affiliate links to iHerb.com using affiliate code GUW6132. When you click these links, iHerb may place cookies on your device to track your purchase for commission purposes. iHerb's privacy policy is available at their website. We receive a small commission if you make a purchase — at no extra cost to you.

GitHub Pages. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server log information. GitHub's privacy policy is available at GitHub's website.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from our email list at any time
  • Object to or restrict processing of your information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at healyourselfguide101@gmail.com.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you are subscribed to our newsletter. When you unsubscribe, your email is removed from our active list. You may also request complete deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
