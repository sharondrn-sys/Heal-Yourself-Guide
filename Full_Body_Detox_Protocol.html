Foundation — Before Anything Else

Clean Water — The Most Important Step

Before supplements. Before dietary changes. Before anything else in this protocol — you need to address your water. Not because water is the most dramatic intervention. Because every single thing you do in this protocol is undermined if the water you drink, cook with, and bathe in is actively working against you.

Standard municipal tap water in most countries contains a chemical cocktail that was never tested for synergistic effects on human health. The individual levels of each contaminant may be declared "safe." Their combined, cumulative, daily exposure over years — that has never been studied. And the list of what's in there is long.

What Is Actually in Your Tap Water

⚠️ What Standard Water Treatment Does Not Remove

Fluoride — added deliberately as a dental public health measure. Documented thyroid disruptor. Accumulates in the pineal gland. Not removed by standard carbon filters. Requires specific filtration (reverse osmosis, activated alumina, bone char).

Chlorine & Chloramines — used to kill pathogens in the distribution system. Continue killing microorganisms — including beneficial gut bacteria — after you drink them. Chloramines are harder to remove than chlorine and require catalytic carbon filters.

Pharmaceutical residues — antibiotics, hormones (including synthetic estrogens from contraceptive pills), antidepressants, blood pressure medications, painkillers. These compounds pass through the human body and enter wastewater. Municipal treatment plants were not designed to remove them. The WHO confirmed existing treatment methods remove only 20–60% of pharmaceutical byproducts. Reverse osmosis removes up to 99%.

Microplastics — a 2017 study tested bottled water from 9 countries and found microplastics in 93% of samples. Tap water contains them too. They have since been found in human blood, lungs, placentas, and brain tissue. Standard carbon pitcher filters (Brita, etc.) do not adequately remove microplastics.

Heavy metals — lead from aging pipe infrastructure, arsenic from groundwater, cadmium and chromium from industrial sources. Directly compete with essential minerals in your body.

PFAS ("forever chemicals") — industrial compounds used in non-stick coatings, food packaging, firefighting foam. Extremely persistent. Linked to thyroid disease, immune suppression, and certain cancers. Increasingly found in groundwater worldwide.4

Your Skin Absorbs Water Too

This is the part most people miss. Drinking filtered water is essential — but if you shower in unfiltered tap water, your skin absorbs chlorine, chloramines, and other compounds directly into your bloodstream. A 10-minute hot shower exposes you to more chlorine absorption than drinking 2 litres of the same water. Hot water opens pores and increases absorption. Steam carries volatile compounds into your lungs.

For this protocol, shower filtration is not optional. It is part of the drainage door work.

Reverse osmosis and distillation remove all dissolved minerals — including beneficial ones like calcium and magnesium. If using RO or distilled water as your main source, add minerals back: a pinch of real sea salt per litre, a remineralisation filter stage, or trace mineral drops. This is why the Morning Sole Drink (real salt in water) in Phase 1 is particularly important if you are using RO water.

Door 5 — The Skin

The skin is the body's largest organ and a significant detoxification route. Sweating excretes heavy metals, BPA, phthalates, and other toxins directly. Most modern people barely sweat at all. This needs to change.

The Healing Detox Bath

Three times per week minimum during Phase 1. Increase to daily if experiencing strong detox symptoms.

No bath? No problem. Use a large foot basin — the same ingredients, the same transdermal absorption, the same effect through the skin of the feet and lower legs. Or purchase a portable inflatable bath tub ($20–40 online) — folds flat for storage, fills with a showerhead. Foot soaking is a legitimate and effective alternative — your feet have some of the highest skin permeability of any body surface.

🛁 Full Detox Bath Recipe
Epsom Salt — 2 cups (magnesium sulfate, supports liver sulfation pathway)
Baking Soda — 1 cup (alkalises water, neutralises chlorine)
Real Sea Salt — ½ cup (full-spectrum minerals)
Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV) — 1 cup (restores skin's acid mantle, antifungal)
Bentonite Clay — ½ cup (binds heavy metals transdermally — premix with water first)
Ground Ginger — 2 tablespoons (opens pores, stimulates sweating)
Draw a warm (not hot) bath. Premix bentonite clay with a small amount of water before adding to the bath — never mix clay directly in dry form.
Add all ingredients and stir well.
Soak for 20–40 minutes. Drink filtered water throughout.
Rest afterward — do not shower for at least one hour. Let the minerals continue absorbing.

💰 Budget version: Epsom salt + baking soda alone — under $5 per bath. This is good enough. Add the other ingredients as budget allows.

🦶 Foot soak version: Use half quantities in a large basin. Soak feet and lower legs for 30–40 minutes. Equally effective for mineral absorption and skin detoxification through one of the body's most permeable surfaces.

🥬 Basic Sauerkraut Recipe
1 medium cabbage, finely shredded
1–2% of cabbage weight in real salt (not iodised)
Shred cabbage finely. Weigh it. Calculate 1–2% of that weight in salt.
Combine cabbage and salt in a large bowl. Massage firmly with hands for 5–10 minutes until liquid releases.
Pack tightly into a clean glass jar. Press down firmly until liquid rises above the cabbage.
Cover loosely (not airtight) and leave at room temperature, away from direct sunlight, for 3–7 days.
Taste from Day 3. When pleasantly sour, seal and refrigerate. Keeps for months.
